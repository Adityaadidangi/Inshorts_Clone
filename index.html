Mumbai records its highest 24-hour October rain in a decade

Mumbai has witnessed its highest 24-hour October rainfall in 10 years as Santacruz weather observatory, representative of the suburbs and Mumbai, recorded 86.5mm if rain between 8:30 am Wednesday and 8:30 am Thursday. Prior to this, the decade's highest one-day October rainfall was recorded in 2011 (October 13) at 71mm.
